What “house detailing” means

House detailing is not the same as regular cleaning. Think of it like car wash vs. car detailing. A standard clean improves what you can see. Detailing restores what’s underneath.

Beyond surface-level

We focus on the areas most services miss — behind, beneath, and around furniture and fixtures.

Systematic, not rushed

Our work is methodical and checklist-led so nothing is overlooked when the job gets detailed.

Finish you can feel

The result is a true “reset” — the kind of clean that changes how the home feels, not just how it looks.

Simple comparison: A standard clean is like a car wash. House detailing is a full detail. It takes longer, costs more, and the difference is obvious.

FAQ

How is this different from regular cleaning?

Regular cleaning is usually surface-level and time-boxed. Detailing is methodical and thorough — like the difference between a car wash and a full car detail.

Do I need to tidy before you arrive?

No. If you’d rather not prep the space, we can take inventory of what’s in the room, detail carefully around items, and return everything properly. That said, fewer items in the space can reduce time and cost.

How often do clients book this service?

Most clients use The Detail Standard once or twice a year — seasonal resets, post-renovation, move-in/move-out, or before/after hosting.

Which areas do you cover?

All London zones.
